Percolation Recipes

Percolation Methods are those that the water only comes in contact with the water that is used for brewing during the time that it's flowing through the method. Usually results in a milder but more delicate brew.


Infusion Methods

Infusion Methods are those that the water is in constant contact with the hot water, thus extracting less nuanced flavors but stronger bodied ones.
